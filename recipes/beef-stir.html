Beef Stir Fry

Description

This tender ground beef stir fry is an easy meal full of fresh veggies, with a flavor-packed sauce that coats every bite. We enjoy this with jasmine rice, but if noodles are your thing, go for it.

Ingredients

Stir Fry

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons oil of your choice
  • 1/2 small head cabbage
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 4 ounces Baby Bella mushrooms

Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons plus 1/4 cup lower-sodium soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup of beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly grated ginger
  • sesame seeds and sliced green onion
