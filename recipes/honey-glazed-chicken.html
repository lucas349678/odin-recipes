Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

This honey chicken dish is easy to make with just 5 ingredients. Delicious served on steamed rice and garnished with sliced green onions.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 tablespoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces
