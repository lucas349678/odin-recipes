Pesto Pasta

Description

This is a quick 15-20 Minute recipe to make some tasty pasta with some pesto of your choice

Ingredients

  • Your favorite Pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Some Pasta of your choice
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • seasoning to your liking

Steps

  1. Cook your Pasta in salted water until its done and drain the water but leave about a tablespoon
  2. Heat up a pan, add the pasta, seasonings and the tablespoon of pasta water
  3. Now add the pasta into the mixture, give it a good stir and enjoy
